- `claude_monitor.py` — Claude API terminal monitoring dashboard built in Python stdlib with curses

### Atmospheric Physics — *The Hammer and The Scalpel*
An original invention concept for atmospheric precipitation control. The system uses **electrostatic polarization of water molecules** followed by a **resonant frequency sweep** to drive droplet coalescence into rainfall. Joey ran a Python optimization simulation across 81 parameter combinations. Optimal configuration: square wave at 8,000 V/m, 100µs pulse, 2.0s gap, with a logarithmic frequency sweep from 312 kHz down to 60 Hz over 2 seconds. This has been compiled into a structured project document.

### Nanoscale Device Research
An experimental concept combining **electret charge**, **quantum tunneling through metal-insulator-metal layers**, and **Brownian motion** into a novel device architecture. Joey designed a homebrew electroplating test protocol, with his children involved as an educational component.

### Traditional Chinese Medicine & Stem Cell Biology
Researched natural foods and Traditional Chinese Medicine herbs that support endogenous stem cell production. Produced a formatted reference document covering Astragalus, Panax Ginseng, Reishi, Cordyceps, and others, with a 7-day meal plan and 4-month onboarding protocol.

### Electronics & PCB Design
Active work in KiCad 8 schematic and PCB layout. Current/recent project: a **4S 18650-powered stepper motor controller board** using Arduino Nano / Seeeduino XIAO, A4988 driver, XL4016 buck converter, and a 16x2 I2C LCD display, targeting ~100×80mm 2-layer FR4.
