## 2. Research & Academic Interests

### Cosmology & Theoretical Physics
Joey's primary research focus is an alternative cosmological framework challenging the standard ΛCDM model. His work is published on Zenodo under the name Joseph Wimsatt:

**"Tired Light Theory: A Unified Framework for Dark Matter, Stellar Anomalies, and Cosmic Structure via Higgs Field Interaction"**
DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.18815508

**Core thesis:** Photons lose energy through interaction with the Higgs field as they travel across cosmological distances — a mechanism that explains redshift without requiring universal expansion. This framework makes specific, testable predictions:
- An effective Hubble parameter (H_eff) derived from Higgs coupling
- A predicted cosmic microwave background temperature of ~2.68 K (vs. the measured 2.725 K)
- Structure in the cosmic microwave background temperature fluctuation spectrum (delta-T/T)
- A unified account of dark matter phenomena, stellar anomalies, and large-scale cosmic structure

**Outreach targets identified:** Eleonora Di Valentino, Sunny Vagnozzi, Stacy McGaugh, Subir Sarkar — researchers known for openness to non-standard cosmology and Hubble tension work.

**Submission venues explored:** Gravity Research Foundation essay contest, FQxI — both open to independent researchers. The Buchalter Cosmology Prize was identified as requiring a PhD.
